The data is obtained from Kaggle - https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/columbine/imdb-dataset-sentiment-analysis-in-csv-format
The dataset is movie reviews taken from "IMDB dataset (Sentiment Analysis) in CSV format"
Dataset contain 3 files:
Train.csv: 40,000 reviews with label 1 or 0
Test.csv: 5,000 reviews with label 1 or 0
Valid.csv: 5,000 reviews with label 1 or 0
